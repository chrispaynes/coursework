  • About UE

    Urban Emerald is a dedicated to showcasing the unique architectural gems found throughout major metropolitan areas. The purpose is to highlight the architecture and share some of the fascinating history that led to the creation of the piece.

    Subscribers can expect to learn about current architectural rarities that pop up each day as well as historic architectural successes.

  • minneapolis

    The buildings and structures that form Minnesota's varied built environment have played a powerful role in shaping the life of the state, serving as places of shelter, work, education, government, ritual, and entertainment. The story of architecture in Minnesota is inseparable from the story of its people—a narrative of growth and achievement interspersed with episodes of destruction and loss.
