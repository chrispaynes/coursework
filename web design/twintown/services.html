USE OUR SERVICES

Twin Town Guitar offers many services. Look below to learn what we offer.

Lessons

We offer music lessons for those looking to develop their guitar, bass, keyboard, and drum skills. Join us for private 60 minute sessions. Twice a month we also offer lessons for those with band instruments like trumpets, clarinets, and saxophones.

Rentals

Are you in a bind? Is your instrument broken? Relax — we have plenty of high-quality instruments for you to borrow. From 24 hours to 24 days, we have you covered. Stop by or call ahead to learn more about our rental options.

Repairs

We get it, instruments break, get out of tune and otherwise need occasional repair. Don't waste time or risk ruining your instrument trying to repair it yourself. Bring it in and we'll inspect your instrument and diagnose the problem while you wait.

Trades

Is it time to upgrade your instrument or your gear? Bring in your gently used instrument and driver's license and we'll offer you a check for your trade in. If you're looking for a new instrument, we'll credit you on your next purchase.
